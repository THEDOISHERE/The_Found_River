Reefback facts

Reefback appearance:

The Reefback is a massive aquatic organism, its skin is dark blue and very thick. To move through the water, the Reefback uses 3 long tentacles.

The Reefback Leviathan is capable of supporting a small scale ecosystem on it's back. Some organisms in the ecosystem are:

  • Biters
  • Bladderfish
  • Boomerang
  • Eyeye
  • Holefish
  • Acid Mushrooms
  • Tiger Plants
  • Violet Beau

Top 3 fun facts about Reefbacks!

  1. Reefbacks are one of the only docile Leviathans!
  2. Reefbacks can live for many centuries!
  3. Just like their Earth counterpart, the whale, Reefbacks can emit low-frequancy calls to communicate!
